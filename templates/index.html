Our Mission

Our Mission

The team eventually was able to raise over 50,000 AED for a project after our last conference that will help provide access to electricity in schools in rural India by building renewable solar panels, an unconventional undertaking as charity projects on behalf of an MUN are often rare, inspiring other students to also make real-world impact.
