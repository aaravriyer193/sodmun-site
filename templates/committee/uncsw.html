The United Nations Commission on the Status of Women (UNCSW) is the principal global intergovernmental body exclusively dedicated to the promotion of gender equality and the empowerment of women. Established in 1946 as a functional commission of ECOSOC, it plays a vital role in shaping global standards, documenting women's realities, and monitoring the implementation of the Beijing Declaration and Platform for Action.
